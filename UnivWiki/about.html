Who We Are

When I arrived here in 2020, I had no idea what I was getting in to I also knew very little about New Orleans and the University however, I knew I wanted to help people and to make a positive impact. Everywhere I went I saw divides and I wanted a way to inform and inspire change. To this end the idea behind this wiki was conceived and in the hopes of realizing that goal we partnered with the Universities IT department to fully realize the dream that is here today.
